Onion Soup

The best Soup of the Nana

This is The List of Ingredients:

  • Â½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices French bread

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter with olive oil in an 8-quart stock pot over medium heat. Add onions to butter and continually stir until tender and translucent.
  3. Add beef broth, sherry, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer).
  5. Place bowls on a cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly, 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
